Core Values

  1. Integrity: Upholding moral principles of respect, decency, truthfulness, and honest in all our actions and decisions.
  2. Transparency: Committing to open and honest communication in executing our duties and operations.
  3. Accountability: Taking ownership and responsibility for our actions and their outcomes while fulfilling our duties.
  4. Confidentiality: Safeguarding priviledged information and respecting the confidentiality of individuals and entities involved in our work is key to driving change in our communities and gain trust as agents of change.
  5. Teamwork: Fostering a culture of collaboration and unity, valuing and leveraging diversity to achieve our common goals.
