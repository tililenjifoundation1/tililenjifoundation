OUR WORKS

  1. We provide access to healthcare services by providing mobile point-to-care kits through the Nthanzi(good health)healthcare clinic, thereby ensuring individuals in underserved communities receive essential healthcare services.
  2. We conduct health awareness programs for communities on various health-related issues including blood donation awareness thus contributing to the achievement of good health and well being of UN sustanable development goal 3.
  3. We advocate for and provide education on safe sex practices to reduce the risk of sexually transmitted infections among the youth populations.
  4. We work to prevent early pregnancies among youth through comprehensive education and support programs, empowering them to make informed decisions about their reproductive health and future.
  5. We provide counselling on various issues that affect mental health as well as training to the disabled and parents with disabled children.
  6. We work towards the reduction of martenal deaths as per UN sustanaible goal 3.1 by increasing blood donation through community awareness on the importance of blood donation in collaboratiion with Chinsali provincial blood bank.
